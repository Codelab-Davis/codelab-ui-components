## Best Practices

When using the Card component, consider these guidelines to ensure a consistent and user-friendly experience:

1. **Clear Structure:** Use a title, description, and actions to keep content structured.
2. **Minimalist Design:** Avoid overcrowding cards with too much content.
3. **Consistent Spacing:** Maintain proper spacing for readability.
4. **Consistent Sizing:** Maintain uniform card sizes within the same interface context.
